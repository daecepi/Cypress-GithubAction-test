2.1 Accordion

Create accordions to display text

Example

Accordion container

keyboard_arrow_down
Accordion title

Accordian paragraph text 
<section class="c-accordion js-accordion">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="c-accordion__container">
      <h2>Accordion container</h2>
      <div class="c-accordion__body">
        <h4 class="js-accordion-toggle c-accordion-title" >
          <div><i class="material-icons notranslate">keyboard_arrow_down</i></div>
          Accordion title
        </h4>
        <div class="c-accordion-content">
        Accordian paragraph text
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

2.2 Cards

This card can have an image, an eyeborw, a title, and a link. They are usually placed in rows of 3

Example

Relationship Era

Read More

  <div class="c-card__entry">
